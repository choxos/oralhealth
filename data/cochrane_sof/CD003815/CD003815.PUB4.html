There were numerous comparisons between different implants that varied by surface preparation, shape, material and type, only 1 of these varying for each comparison. Most of the comparisons were single study. There was only 1 statistically significant difference for bone level change from 1 single study, which indicated more bone loss for Nobel Active than Nobel Speedy Groovy (MD 0.59 mm; 95% CI 0.44 to 0.74)
